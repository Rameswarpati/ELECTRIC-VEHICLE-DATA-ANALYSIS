Electric Vehicle Data Analysis Dashboard | Tableau

Designed an end-to-end interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing 1,50,000+ electric vehicles across multiple dimensions including model year, state, make, EV type, and CAFV eligibility.

Analyzed key KPIs such as Average Electric Range (67.8 miles), BEV vs PHEV distribution, and year-over-year growth trends to identify market adoption patterns.

Built dynamic filters (EV Type, State, CAFV Eligibility, Model) enabling stakeholders to perform drill-down analysis for targeted decision-making.

Visualized EV adoption growth from 2011–2024, highlighting peak growth years and recent market shifts.

Created geographic heat maps to compare state-wise EV distribution, supporting regional demand analysis.

Identified top manufacturers and models, revealing Tesla’s market dominance (52%+ share) and top-performing models like Model Y and Model 3.

Analyzed CAFV eligibility impact, categorizing vehicles into Eligible, Not Eligible, and Unknown segments to support policy and incentive analysis.

Delivered actionable insights for business and policy stakeholders by converting raw data into clear, visually compelling dashboards.
